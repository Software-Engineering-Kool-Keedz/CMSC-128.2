Depression AI Software (DAISo) is developed to enhance the user interface of the explainableAI of a machine learning project focusing on the diagnosis of depression and anxiety based on a number of factors. The objective of this website is to provide more effective visualization of the machine learning results that will prove useful for aiding physicians in comprehending the diagnosis of depression for their patients.

American Psychiatric Association describes depression as a recurrent and serious medical illness that affects a person's emotions, feelings, thoughts, and actions negatively. Data science can play a significant role in helping predict the risk of illnessess such as depression.
In a study by V.P. Magboo and M.S. Magboo, several machine learning techniques were applied on a depression dataset. Each model was trained and tested on the same sets, and performance metrics were obtained to rank the machine learning models and get the highest-performing one. The top performing model was found to be logistic regression with excellent performance metrics. Feature importance scores of the most important attributes were generated for the best model.
Thus, DAISo utilizes Logistic Regression in predicting the risk of depression among patients.
