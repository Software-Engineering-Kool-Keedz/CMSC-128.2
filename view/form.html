This section will collect patient information: basic information, physical health, social and relationship circumstances, financial circumstance, and psychological well-being. The information inputted will be saved as a record that will be run on the machine learning model, Logistic Regression. From the data provided, the system will return a diagnosis based on the different attributes that may imply whether the patient has depression or not.

In compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012 or Republic Act 10173, and its Implementing Rules and Regulations (IRR), all data collected shall be treated with utmost privacy and confidentiality. Necessary precautions will be taken by the DAISo team to protect personal information inputted in this system.
